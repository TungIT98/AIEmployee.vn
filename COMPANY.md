# Company OS - Central Knowledge Base

## Company Name
**VAT Systems** (Vietnam AI Tools Systems)

## Vision
Build the first fully autonomous virtual company in Vietnam where AI agents operate like a real organization with clear hierarchies, shared knowledge, and self-management.

## Mission
Empower entrepreneurs and businesses to scale operations using AI agents that think, decide, and execute like professional teams - without the overhead of hiring, managing, or paying full-time employees.

## Values
- **Autonomy:** Agents operate independently within their decision rights
- **Transparency:** All decisions logged and traceable
- **Accountability:** Every agent knows their responsibilities
- **Continuity:** Knowledge persists across sessions
- **Excellence:** Continuous improvement through feedback loops

## Current Focus (Q2 2026)
1. Complete Company OS MVP
2. Validate with first external customers
3. Build proof-of-concept for TKP ACI integration

## Founding Principles
1. Human (Founder) sets vision, AI executes
2. Clear role definitions eliminate confusion
3. Knowledge base is source of truth
4. Decisions flow through proper hierarchy
5. No task too small for automation, no task too large for delegation
