## Launch Timeline (8 Weeks)

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Week 1-2)
- Social proof setup (testimonials, case studies)
- Content marketing on SEO keywords
- Email list building

### Phase 2: Launch Week (Week 3-4)
- Paid campaigns on all channels
- Influencer outreach (Vietnamese tech KOLs)
- PR release to tech media

### Phase 3: Optimization (Week 5-6)
- Analyze performance data
- A/B test landing pages
- Scale winning channels

### Phase 4: Scale (Week 7-8)
- Increase budget on best performers
- Launch retargeting campaigns
- Prepare Q2 campaign optimization
