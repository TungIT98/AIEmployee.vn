## Product Overview

KeToanAI is an AI-powered accounting assistant that delivers real-time financial insights, automates compliance workflows, and eliminates the month-end accounting crunch — from invoice to journal entry, fully automated.

**Primary Target:** Accounting Firms, SME Owners
**Secondary:** Enterprise (cross-sell with TKP ACI)

## Key Value Propositions

- **Real-Time Financial Insights** — Dashboards that update as transactions happen, not at month-end close
- **Automated Compliance** — Continuous compliance monitoring;KeToanAI flags issues as they arise, not in retrospectives
- **Continuous Close Cycle** — From invoice (via TKP ACI) to journal entry to financial statement in real-time — no more month-end crunch
- **AI-Powered Accuracy** — Machine learning continuously improves categorization and reconciliation accuracy over time
