## Battle Card 1: AIEmployee.vn vs Traditional BPO

### Positioning
**Our Message:** "AI employees that work 24/7 at 20% of the cost — no sick days, no turnover, no compliance headaches."

| Factor | Traditional BPO | AIEmployee.vn |
|--------|----------------|---------------|
| **Monthly Cost** | 15-30M VND/employee | 199K-999K VND/month |
| **Availability** | 8 hours/day (5 days/week) | 24/7/365 |
| **Language Support** | Limited to hired staff | 3+ languages, always available |
| **Scaling Speed** | 2-4 weeks hiring/onboarding | Instant deployment |
| **Consistency** | Variable (human error, turnover) | 100% consistent |
| **Data Security** | Human risk, requires NDA enforcement | Enterprise-grade security |
| **Training Cost** | Ongoing | None — pre-trained |
| **Multi-tasking** | 1-2 tasks per person | Unlimited concurrent tasks |

### Key Talking Points
1. **Cost:** "An AI employee costs less than 1 week of a traditional employee's salary per month."
2. **Availability:** "Your AI employee never calls in sick, never takes vacation, never clocks out early."
3. **Quality:** "Every interaction is consistent, documented, and measurable."
4. **Speed:** "Scale from 1 to 100 AI employees in the time it takes to approve a purchase order."

### Pricing Comparison
```
Traditional BPO:
- 1 Customer Service Rep: 12-18M VND/month (salary) + 3-5M VND (benefits, office)
- 1 Data Entry Clerk: 10-15M VND/month + 2-4M VND (overheads)
- Annual cost for 1 CSR: ~200M+ VND

AIEmployee.vn:
- Starter: 199K/month — Basic tasks, 1 AI employee
- Professional: 499K/month — Advanced tasks, 3 AI employees
- Enterprise: 999K/month — Full suite, unlimited AI employees
- Annual cost: 2.4M - 12M VND
```

| Objection | Response |
