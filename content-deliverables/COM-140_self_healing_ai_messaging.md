#### Fault Tolerance Architecture
Self-Healing AI is built on a three-layer resilience model:

1. **Detection Layer** — Real-time monitoring of model inputs, outputs, and system health metrics. Triggers alerts at 85% of normal threshold, auto-remediation at 70%.
2. **Correction Layer** — Autonomous decision engine that selects from pre-approved remediation actions: workflow reroute, model rollback, data imputation, or human escalation.
3. **Learning Layer** — Post-incident analysis that updates the correction playbook automatically, ensuring each recovery makes the system more resilient.

## ROI Calculator Concepts

### ROI Calculator #1: Downtime Cost Calculator

**Inputs:**
- Average hourly revenue affected by AI downtime ($)
- Current AI platform uptime SLA (%)
- Average AI-related incident frequency (per month)
- Average time to resolve incidents (hours)
- IT team hours spent on AI incidents (per week)

**Outputs:**
- Current annual cost of AI failures
- Projected cost with Self-Healing AI (99.99% uptime)
- Annual savings
- Payback period

**Sample Calculation:**
```
Inputs:
- Hourly revenue at risk: $5,000
- Current uptime: 97% = ~262 hours downtime/year
- Incidents/month: 8
- Avg resolution time: 2 hours
- IT hours/week on AI issues: 15

Current Annual Cost:
= (262 hrs × $5,000) + (8 × 12 × 2 hrs × $5,000) + (15 × 52 × $75/hr)
= $1,310,000 + $960,000 + $58,500
= $2,328,500/year

With Self-Healing AI (99.99% uptime):
= 8.76 hrs × $5,000 + minimal IT overhead
= ~$44,000/year

Annual Savings: ~$2,284,500
```

**Outputs:**
