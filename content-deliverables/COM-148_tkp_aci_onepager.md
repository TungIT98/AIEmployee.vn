## Product Overview

TKP ACI (E-Invoice & Compliance Integration) automates Vietnam's e-invoice workflows — from invoice creation to tax authority submission — eliminating manual data entry, compliance errors, and the risk of penalties.

**Primary Target:** Accounting Firms, Tax/Compliance-focused businesses
**Secondary:** SME Owners, Enterprise (cross-sell with AIEmployee.vn)

---

## Key Value Propositions

- **Fully Compliant** — Built to meet Vietnam's e-invoice (Hóa Đơn Điện Tử) regulations; automatic updates as tax laws evolve
- **Automated Error-Free Processing** — No manual data entry; invoices flow directly from your ERP or sales system to the tax authority
- **Seamless ERP Integration** — API-first design connects to existing accounting systems in hours, not weeks
- **Cross-Sell with AIEmployee** — AIEmployee handles sales transactions; TKP ACI automatically invoices them — zero manual handoff
