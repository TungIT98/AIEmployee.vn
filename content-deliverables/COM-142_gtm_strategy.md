## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This document defines the unified go-to-market strategy for VAT Systems' multi-product ecosystem:
- **AIEmployee.vn** - Virtual AI employees for business operations
- **TKP ACI** - Vietnamese Tax/Kế Toán/Accounting Integration (E-invoice & Compliance)
- **TKP Dev Studio** - Developer productivity suite
- **KeToanAI** - AI-powered accounting assistant

### Strategic Vision
Position VAT Systems as Vietnam's premier "AI-Native Business Platform" - a unified ecosystem where AI employees handle operations, compliance, and development.

### Target Market Segments
| Segment | Primary Product | TAM | Priority |
|---------|---------------|-----|----------|
| SME Owners (10-200 employees) | AIEmployee.vn | 50,000 businesses | HIGH |
| Accounting Firms | KeToanAI + TKP ACI | 5,000 firms | HIGH |
| Enterprise (200+ employees) | Full Suite | 2,000 businesses | MEDIUM |
| SaaS Startups | TKP Dev Studio | 3,000 startups | MEDIUM |
| Tax/Compliance-focused | TKP ACI | All above | CROSS-SELL |

## 3. CROSS-PRODUCT CAMPAIGN STRATEGY

### Campaign Architecture

```
Quarter 2 2026 - "AI Transformation" Campaign
├── Phase 1 (April): AIEmployee.vn Launch
├── Phase 2 (May): TKP ACI Integration
├── Phase 3 (June): KeToanAI Beta
└── Phase 4 (July): TKP Dev Studio Preview
```

### Integrated Campaign Flow

**Month 1-2: AIEmployee.vn Dominance**
- Launch content: "AI Employee for Every Business"
- Target: SME owners, Founders, HR Tech decision makers
- Channels: LinkedIn, Facebook, Google Ads
- Budget: 60% of total

**Month 3-4: Compliance Bundle**
- Cross-sell message: "AI Employees + TKP ACI = Full Automation"
- Target: Existing AIEmployee customers + new compliance-focused
- Channels: Email sequences, webinar, case studies
- Budget: 25% of total

**Month 5-6: Ecosystem Expansion**
- Launch: KeToanAI (Accounting AI)
- Preview: TKP Dev Studio
- Target: Accounting firms, Developer communities
- Budget: 15% of total

### Cross-Sell Logic

```
AIEmployee.vn Customer
       │
       ├──► Downsell need: "Tax compliance is manual"
       │         │
       │         └──► Add TKP ACI (E-invoice automation)
       │
       ├──► Upsell need: "Need smarter accounting"
       │         │
       │         └──► Add KeToanAI (Accounting AI)
       │
       └──► Technical need: "Build custom integrations"
                 │
                 └──► Pilot TKP Dev Studio
```

## 4. ENTERPRISE SALES ENABLEMENT

### Enterprise Sales Playbook

**Stage 1: Awareness (Week 1-2)**
- LinkedIn thought leadership: "AI in Vietnam: 2026 State of Play"
- Content: Industry reports, case studies (PDF gated)
- Touch: 1 cold outreach per contact

**Stage 2: Interest (Week 3-4)**
- Webinar: "How SME 500+ employees cut ops costs 40% with AI"
- Content: ROI calculator, TCO comparison tool
- Touch: 2 follow-ups, demo scheduling

**Stage 3: Consideration (Week 5-8)**
- Free pilot: 30-day AIEmployee trial
- Technical workshop: IT integration assessment
- Touch: Weekly check-ins, success metrics review

**Stage 4: Decision (Week 9-12)**
- Custom pricing proposal
- Security/compliance review
- Reference customer calls (2-3)
- Touch: Daily engagement until signature
